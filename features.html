Features of FindDroid

FindDroid offers a comprehensive set of features designed to help you locate and secure your lost phone. Explore the functionalities that make FindDroid an essential tool for any Android user.

Location Tracking

Track your phone’s location in real-time using GPS. Whether your phone is lost in your home or in a different city, FindDroid helps you pinpoint its exact location.

Anti Power Off

Prevent your phone from being powered off by unauthorized users. FindDroid ensures your device remains active and trackable, even if someone tries to turn it off.

Ringing in Silent Mode

Make your phone ring loudly even if it’s set to silent or vibrate mode. This feature is perfect for when your phone is misplaced in a noisy environment or hidden under objects.

Control via WhatsApp, Telegram, and SMS

Send commands to your phone using popular messaging apps like WhatsApp and Telegram, or via traditional SMS. You can remotely control your device, trigger alarms, and more.

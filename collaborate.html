Collaborate With Us

At FindDroid, we believe in working together to build better solutions. If you’re passionate about development, design, or simply want to help spread the word, here's how you can get involved:

Contribute to Development

Join our development efforts to add new features or improve the existing functionality. We're open to ideas that enhance the app’s ability to help people find their phones.

Design and UI/UX

Help us improve the user interface. If you have experience in UI/UX design, your input can make FindDroid more intuitive and easy to use.

Spread the Word

You can help by sharing FindDroid with your friends, family, and on social media. The more people who know about it, the more we can help.

Email us at finddroid@protonmail.com to get started.
